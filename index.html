Welcome

This handbook is your new best friend. It's a living document, a toolkit, and a roadmap designed to get you to the final tribal council. It's built on a simple premise: knowledge is power. The more you know about the game, the better you'll play.

SURVIVOR NOTEBOOKLM ↗

The Survivor NotebookLM is your private, AI-powered workspace to strategize, analyze, and prepare. Use it to break down challenges, simulate tribal councils, and develop your winning narrative. It's your secret weapon.

Key Features

This handbook is designed to be a comprehensive, interactive resource.

  • Interactive Cards: Click on any topic to dive deeper.
  • Dynamic Timeline: Easily navigate through different phases of the game.
  • Content-Rich: Packed with strategies, historical analysis, and survival tips.

About the Website

This handbook was created by a long-time fan of the show, combining a passion for game theory and a deep respect for the castaways who brave the elements and the social gauntlet.
