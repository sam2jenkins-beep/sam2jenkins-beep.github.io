About

A Gift

This handbook is my early Christmas gift to you as you gear up for the biggest adventure of your life!

No Stress Required

Seriously, please don't let this book stress you out or feel like more homework or feel obligated to use it in any certain way. The goal here is quite literally the opposite. I know you're already a Survivor guru in your heart and mind, so this is just a token of support, and go-to resource to give you a place to:

  • Prep Your Mind: Get an outlet for some fun research and start mentally preparing for all the awesome craziness that's coming your way.
  • Feel Totally Ready: Give you some confidence and a few strategies for things you can start doing right now before you leave.
  • Breathe Easier: I want nothing more than for you to feel confident, prepared, and ready to tackle anything the moment you land!

Think of this as a big brutish virtual hug filled with guidelines, excitement, and absolutely zero pressure.
